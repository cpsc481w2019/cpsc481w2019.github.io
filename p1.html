Project

The project we are proposing is a Pet Adoption Website.

The website will provide a page listing animals available for adoption, as well as information and pictures of each animal. The system would be used by people of all ages that would be interested in adopting a pet, and will direct them to the proper connections to get the adoption process started.

Stakeholders and Users

Stakeholders: Visitors, caretakers, adopters, people who surrending their pets.

Users: Visitors, adopters, people who surrending their pets.
